AZA Elephant Welfare Initiative

The AZA Elephant Welfare Initiative is a collaborative effort toward using evidenced-based methods and software technology to inform and enhance elephant care. The Initiative builds upon the knowledge gained through the Using Science to Understand Zoo Elephant Welfare Study and places the AZA elephant community at the forefront of scientifically informed animal care.
